About Us

Founded in 1998, DR lankapalli bullaya College aims to deliver industry-relevant education with a focus on research, entrepreneurship and community outreach. Our campus hosts modern labs, a central library, and partnerships with local businesses.

Admissions

How to Apply

Submit an online application, upload transcripts, and attend an interview (if required). Scholarships available for meritorious students.

  • Step 1: Fill application form online
  • Step 2: Submit supporting documents
  • Step 3: Entrance test / Interview

Fees & Scholarships

Transparent fee structure with multiple scholarship categories (merit, need-based, sports, research assistantships).

Contact admissions@lankapalli.edu for fee schedules and scholarship forms.
